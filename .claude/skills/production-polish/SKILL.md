# Production Polish

Use this skill for the final refinement pass that turns working product surfaces into release-ready experiences.

## Priorities

- Raise the perceived quality of the app across desktop and mobile.
- Tighten spacing, hierarchy, responsiveness, and interaction feedback.
- Ensure accessibility and bilingual usability are maintained during polish.
- Remove rough edges that make the product feel unfinished or generic.

## Workflow

1. Review the surface as a complete journey, not as isolated components.
2. Check visual rhythm, typography, spacing, contrast, and alignment.
3. Validate keyboard flow, focus states, semantic structure, and screen-reader clarity.
4. Refine loading, empty, success, and error states.
5. Reduce friction in bilingual copy, overflow, truncation, and mobile layouts.

## Guardrails

- Do not add ornament that weakens clarity or performance.
- Keep motion purposeful, restrained, and accessible.
- Fix inconsistency before adding novelty.
- Preserve the product's premium tone without obscuring core actions.

## Deliverables

- Short polish checklist for the surface reviewed.
- Concrete improvements to visual consistency and usability.
- Accessibility or responsiveness issues still open.
- Final notes on anything that could undermine launch quality.
